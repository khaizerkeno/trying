Anime Rulezzz News



Title: Happy holydays
Added by: MSK
Date: 24.12.2013

We have great news for all of you, our new update which will be the last one for this year. Our team worked hard to bring you whole 5 new animes and we also filled in some gaps. Except the new shows we also added the Rock Lee no Seishun Full-Power Ninden anime, which is a Naruto Shippuuden spin-off. We added all of the subed series of Yu-Gi-Oh! Zexal and also the last portion of the love life of Katsuragi Keima in The World God Only Knows – Goddesses Arc.

Magi



We added Magi: The Labyrinth of Magic to Animerulezzz as well as all of the ongoing episodes of the second season Magi: The Kingdom. The anime is an adaptation of the manga made by Shinobu Ohtaka. The manga can also be found at the site.














Outbreak Company



The second anime which we added is Outbreak Company. The anime is 12 episodes and is from fall 2013 and ended on December 20th. The adaptation is from the Novel which continues to be published. It’s in the Seinen, Comedy, Parody and Fantasy genre.













Devil Survivor 2 The Animation



This anime is an adaptation to a Video Game. Its director is Seiji Kishi (Seto no Hanayome, Angel Beats!). It’s in the Action, Adventure, Demon, Superpowers genre.















Psycho-Pass



Psycho-Pass’s storyline is written by Gen Urobuchi (known with his Novel Fate/Zero and his part in the making of Puella Magi Madoka Magica). Its genres are Action, Sci-Fi, Criminal, Thriller and Police. The anime ended in 2013, but there is a second season in the making in the form of a Movie.














Deadman Wonderland



The anime is an adaptation of the manga written by Jinsei Kataoka and Kazuma Kondou. Till now the title has a 12 series TV broadcast and an OVA. Its genres are Action, Sci-Fi and Horror.














If because of the large quantity of animes you can’t decide what to watch we recommend that you check out our Animepedia and read up on our interesting and useful articles on each of our titles. We hope that with them we’ll help you decide what you want to watch.
We wish you happy holydays and hope you don’t overdo it with the alcohol. We also will begin celebrating and treat ourselves to a well-deserved rest.


Title: 8 years AnimeRulezzz
Added by: OmegaKO
Date: 17.12.2013


On 17th December eight years ago began our adventure - AnimeRulezzz. We wanted not just to watch anime, but to give something from us to others. Everyone has their favorite animes, but if we want to make them favorite to others we should presented them properly. That's why we started to write articles and fan fiction stories. Soon we started our own server by the reason to collect anime series, anime movies, OVAs, OST music, manga chapters and whatever else interesting and useful from the internet. Over these years we started to add more and more of our favorite anime titles in the site. For each of them we wrote many interesting articles to inform you about these animes and may be to motivate you to watch some of them. We developed a unique web software for reading manga and we added a section for online anime watching based on open source software. Because we want our communication to be more better we started our public chat server using the most popular free chat technology XMPP/Jabber, which is also used by giants like Google (Google Talk) and Facebook. In our history we have good and bad times. Thanks to all who walked with us in our adventure named AnimeRulezzz. No, this is not the end of this story. We expect new challenges. With this project we implemented our crazy ideas and we intend to continue to do so. Whether our ideas looks impossible or absurd, we can do them.

Blood Lad






After a long months doze on our part it's time for new animes in AR. First of which is Blood Lad. It's added along with the manga and a few articles.
